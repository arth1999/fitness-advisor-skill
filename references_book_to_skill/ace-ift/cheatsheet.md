# ACE Personal Trainer -- Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

## ACE IFT Model at a Glance

| | Phase 1 | Phase 2 | Phase 3 | Phase 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Functional Movement & Resistance** | Stability & Mobility | Movement Training | Load Training | Performance Training |
| **Cardiorespiratory Training** | Aerobic Base | Aerobic Efficiency | Anaerobic Endurance | Anaerobic Power |
| **Continuous Model** | FUNCTION | FUNCTION->HEALTH | HEALTH->FITNESS | FITNESS->PERFORMANCE |

## MHR Formulas (ACSM Recommended)
- **Gellish**: 206.9 - (0.67 x age) -- SD ~7 bpm
- **Tanaka**: 208 - (0.7 x age)
- **DO NOT USE**: 220 - age (SD ~12 bpm)

## Karvonen Target Heart Rate
```
Target HR = [(MHR - RHR) x %Intensity] + RHR
```

## CAD Risk Factors (ACSM)
| Risk Factor | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Age | Men >= 45; Women >= 55 |
| Family history | MI or sudden death: male 1st-degree < 55; female < 65 |
| Smoking | Current or quit within 6 months |
| Sedentary | < 30 min moderate activity, 3 days/week, 3+ months |
| Hypertension | SBP >= 140 or DBP >= 90 mmHg, or on medication |
| Dyslipidemia | LDL >= 130 or HDL < 40 mg/dL, or on medication |
| Prediabetes | Fasting glucose >= 100 mg/dL |
| Obesity | BMI >= 30 or waist > 102 cm (M) / > 88 cm (F) |
| **NEGATIVE factor** | HDL >= 60 mg/dL (subtract 1) |

## Risk Stratification
- **Low**: <= 1 risk factor, asymptomatic
- **Moderate**: >= 2 risk factors, asymptomatic
- **High**: Known disease OR signs/symptoms

## ACSM FITT-VP Guidelines Summary

### Aerobic Exercise
| Variable | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Frequency | >= 5 days/week moderate OR >= 3 days/week vigorous |
| Intensity | Moderate and/or vigorous |
| Time | 30-60 min/day moderate; 20-60 min/day vigorous |
| Volume | >= 500-1,000 MET-min/week |
| Mode | Continuous or accumulated (>= 10-min bouts) |

### Resistance Training
| Variable | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Frequency | 2-3 days/week per muscle group |
| Intensity | 60-70% 1-RM (novice strength); 40-50% (older beginners); < 50% (endurance) |
| Reps | 8-12 (strength); 10-15 (older); 15-20 (endurance) |
| Sets | 2-4 (strength); 1-2 (older/novice) |
| Rest | 2-3 min between sets; >= 48h between sessions |

## Talk Test Zones
- **Below VT1**: Speak comfortably, full sentences -- Zone 1, Phase 1
- **At VT1**: Speaking becomes difficult, short sentences -- Zone 2, Phase 2
- **At/Above VT2**: Cannot speak more than 1-2 words -- Zone 3, Phase 3
- **Maximal**: Cannot speak at all -- Zone 4, Phase 4 (athletes only)

## McGill Core Ratios
- Flexion:Extension < 1.0
- Right Bridge:Left Bridge = 1.0 +/- 0.05
- Side Bridge:Extension < 0.75

## Five Primary Movement Patterns
1. Bend-and-Lift (squat, hip hinge)
2. Single-Leg (lunge, step-up)
3. Push (horizontal, vertical)
4. Pull (horizontal, vertical)
5. Rotational (spiral)

## Movement Teaching Progression
1. Tell -> Show -> Do -> Feedback
2. Part-to-whole -> Whole
3. Static -> Dynamic -> Loaded -> Powerful

## Stability -> Mobility Progression
1. Proximal stability (core activation, drawing-in, bracing)
2. Static stability (planks, bridges)
3. Dynamic stability (limb movement + core stable)
4. Integrated stability (full-body + core)

## Periodization Types
- **Linear**: Intensity up, volume down over time
- **Undulating**: Daily/weekly variation in volume/intensity
- **Block**: Focused 2-4 week blocks on specific qualities

## Scope of Practice -- Quick Check
**CAN DO**: Design exercise programs, conduct fitness assessments, teach technique, provide general nutrition info (MyPlate), coach behavior change, refer to other professionals

**CANNOT DO**: Diagnose, prescribe diets/supplements, treat injuries, provide rehab, counsel psychologically, work with patients (only clients)

## Emergency Quick Actions
- **Choking**: Conscious -> Heimlich; Unconscious -> CPR
- **Heart attack**: EMS, rest, aspirin (if appropriate)
- **Stroke**: FAST (Face, Arms, Speech, Time)
- **Seizure**: Clear area, protect head, do NOT restrain, do NOT put anything in mouth
- **Acute injury**: PRICE (Protect, Restrict, Ice, Compression, Elevation)

## TTM Behavior Change Stages
| Stage | Timeframe | Trainer Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| Precontemplation | No intent in 6 months | Education, empathy |
| Contemplation | Intent in 6 months | Explore pros/cons, build confidence |
| Preparation | Intent in 30 days | Concrete plan, realistic goals |
| Action | < 6 months active | Relapse prevention, reinforcement |
| Maintenance | >= 6 months active | Variety, independence, lapse planning |

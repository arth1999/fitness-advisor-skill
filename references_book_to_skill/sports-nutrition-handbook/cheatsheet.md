# Sports Nutrition Quick-Reference Cheat Sheet

## Daily Macronutrient Targets

| Nutrient | Sedentary | Light Training | Moderate Training | Heavy Training | Extreme Training |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **CHO (g/kg/d)** | 3-5 | 3-5 | 5-7 | 6-10 | 8-12 |
| **Protein (g/kg/d)** | 0.8 | 1.2-1.4 | 1.4-1.7 | 1.6-2.0 | 1.5-1.8 (endurance) |
| **Fat (% energy)** | 20-35 | 20-35 | 20-35 | 20-35 | 20-35 |

## Carbohydrate Timing Around Exercise

| When | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-exercise (1-4 h) | 1-4 g/kg | Less CHO closer to exercise |
| During (<30 min) | None needed | — |
| During (30-75 min) | Small amounts or mouth rinse | CNS-mediated benefit |
| During (1-2 h) | 30 g/h | Single or multiple transportable |
| During (2-3 h) | 60 g/h | Single or multiple transportable |
| During (>2.5 h) | Up to 90 g/h | Multiple transportable CHO required |
| Post-exercise (0-4 h) | 1.0-1.2 g/kg/h | Add 0.25-0.3 g/kg protein |

## Carbohydrate Loading

| Protocol | Duration | Training | CHO Intake |
|---|---|---|---|
| Classic | Days 1-3: Deplete | Hard training | Low CHO |
| | Days 4-6: Load | Taper | 10 g/kg/d |
| **Modern** | **36-48 h** | **Rest** | **10-12 g/kg/d** |

## Protein Per Meal

| Population | g/kg/meal | Leucine/meal | Example (70 kg) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Young adults | 0.24-0.30 | 2-3 g | 17-21 g protein |
| Masters athletes | 0.40 | 3+ g | 28 g protein |
| Meal frequency: 4-5 meals/day, evenly distributed | | | |

## Hydration Quick Reference

| Timing | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Pre-exercise (4 h) | 5-7 mL/kg body weight |
| Pre-exercise (2 h) | Additional 3-5 mL/kg if no urine |
| During exercise | Replace sweat loss; 0.4-0.8 L/h general guideline |
| Post-exercise | 1.25-1.5 L per 1 kg BW lost, with sodium |
| Sweat rate formula | (Pre-BW g - Post-BW g + Fluid mL - Urine mL) / min |

## Body Composition Change Targets

| Goal | Weekly Rate | Energy Adjustment (Men) | Energy Adjustment (Women) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fat loss | ~0.7% BW | -300 to -500 kcal/d | -200 to -300 kcal/d |
| Lean gain | 0.25-0.75 lb (0.1-0.3 kg) | +400 to +500 kcal/d | +300 to +400 kcal/d |
| Recomposition | Lean gain + fat loss | +300 kcal/d | +200 kcal/d |

## Evidence-Based Ergogenic Aids (AIS Group A)

| Supplement | Protocol | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Caffeine** | 3-6 mg/kg, 60 min pre | Power, endurance, alertness |
| **Creatine** | Load 20 g/d × 5-7 d; 3-5 g/d maintenance | Repeated sprints, strength |
| **β-Alanine** | 3.2-6.4 g/d, split doses, 4+ weeks | Efforts lasting 1-4 min |
| **Bicarbonate** | 0.3 g/kg, 60-90 min pre | Events 1-7 min; GI risk |
| **Beetroot juice/Nitrate** | 5-8 mmol nitrate, 2-3 h pre | Endurance, economy |
| **CHO mouth rinse** | Rinse 5-10 sec, no swallowing | 30-75 min high-intensity |

## Key Micronutrient Red Flags

| Nutrient | At-Risk Groups | Screening | Deficiency Signs |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Iron** | Female, endurance, vegetarian, altitude | Ferritin, Hb, Hct, transferrin sat | Fatigue, ↓ performance, pallor |
| **Vitamin D** | Indoor sport, northern latitude, dark skin | 25(OH)D | Muscle weakness, bone pain, fatigue |
| **Calcium** | Female, amenorrheic, dairy-free | Dietary assessment; BMD (DEXA) | Stress fractures, ↓ BMD |
| **B-12** | Vegan, vegetarian, older adults | Serum B-12, MMA | Anemia, neuropathy, fatigue |

## Female Athlete Triad / RED-S Screening

| System | Assessment | Red Flags |
|---|---|---|
| Energy availability | Diet recall, weight history, eating attitudes | Restricted intake, food rules, weight loss |
| Menstrual function | Age of menarche, cycle frequency | Amenorrhea, oligomenorrhea, delayed menarche |
| Bone health | Stress fracture history, DEXA if indicated | Recurrent fractures, low BMD (Z-score <-1) |
| In males: low libido, low testosterone, low BMD | | |

## At-Risk Sports for Disordered Eating

**Highest risk**: Wrestling, gymnastics, figure skating, diving, dance, bodybuilding, lightweight rowing, jockeys, distance running

## GI Distress Prevention During Exercise

| Strategy | Details |
|---|---|
| Gut training | Gradually increase CHO intake during training |
| Pre-exercise CHO | Low fat, low fiber, low-moderate protein |
| During exercise | Isotonic/hypotonic beverages preferred |
| Avoid triggers | Individualized; common: high fat, fiber, fructose, concentrated CHO |
| Low FODMAP | May help GI-sensitive athletes |

## Recovery Nutrition Timing

| Time Between Sessions | Strategy |
|---|---|
| <8 hours | CHO immediately: 1.0-1.2 g/kg/h + 20-25 g protein; every 15-30 min |
| 8-24 hours | CHO + protein within 1-2 h; then regular meals |
| >24 hours | Regular balanced meals; timing less critical |

## Supplement Safety Quick Check

1. Is the product **NSF Certified for Sport**, **Informed Sport**, or **BSCG Certified Drug Free**?
2. Are all ingredients listed and recognizable?
3. Has it been checked against the **WADA Prohibited List**?
4. Is there evidence of benefit for the specific sport/event?
5. Is the cost justified compared with food alternatives?
